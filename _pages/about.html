Julian leads an engineering team at Amazon. He studied computer science and finance at UT Austin. For more details, consult his (slightly dated) Resume.

Outside of work, he likes to cook, eat, and spend time with friends and family. He likes to dance swing, hike mountains, and travel the world. He likes praying in old Gothic churches, playing tunes with the help of his trusty Yamaha F-310, and keeping time with his classic Casio F-91W-1.

He also likes to write and share a few of his Musings, which are sometimes poetic, sometimes rambly, but always thoroughly honest. He can wax sentimental at times, but he always grounds himself in reality.
